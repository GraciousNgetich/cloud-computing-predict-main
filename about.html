About


My Story: Passion for Curiosity and Practicality

I have always been intrigued by how things work and thoroughly enjoyed hands-on practical experiences. Throughout my academic journey, I excelled in sciences and mathematics, often finding myself among the few women in these fields. Additionally, I naturally gravitated towards leadership roles, navigating a male-dominated world with confidence.

After completing my campus studies, I faced challenges in finding suitable employment, which led me to explore alternative paths. Determined to chart my own course, I decided to learn web development and dedicated two years to honing my skills in that domain. However, my passion for addressing challenges faced by women drove me to dig deeper.


The Quest for "How"

During my pursuit of solutions, I stumbled upon the captivating world of data science. I delved into research on this subject and learned various data analysis techniques and visualization methods. While data science provided answers to my "why" questions, my thirst for understanding the "how" persisted. This is when I discovered the realm of data engineering.

Enthralled by the intricacies of building data infrastructure and processing vast amounts of information, I enrolled in the Explore Data Science Academy. It proved to be the perfect platform to expand my knowledge and expertise in data engineering. Now, I am devoted to managing data infrastructure, blending the "why" and "how" aspects seamlessly.


A Vision for The Future

My primary goal is to leverage my skills in data engineering to assist businesses in making data-driven decisions. I firmly believe that data is the key to unlocking valuable insights and optimizing processes across various industries. Moreover, I am passionate about empowering women to embrace the countless opportunities available in the expansive world of data and technology.

Through my journey, I aspire to be a role model for other women, proving that they too can excel in the tech industry. By combining my leadership qualities with my technical acumen, I hope to make a significant impact on both the data landscape and the lives of aspiring women in technology.
